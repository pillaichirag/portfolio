Hello! I’m Chirag, a data analyst with a unique background in digital marketing, where my hands-on experience with analytics sparked my interest in data. Throughout my marketing career, I often made logical, data-driven decisions to optimize campaigns and enhance client results—long before I knew I wanted to be a data analyst. This exposure led me to dive deeper into data analysis, where I’ve developed skills in Excel, SQL, Power BI, and Python to explore real-world problems through data.

As a quick learner and strong communicator, I’m passionate about continuously expanding my knowledge and uncovering insights that drive meaningful outcomes. My projects range from analyzing IMDb movie data to investigating hiring process metrics, each strengthening my analytical and problem-solving abilities. I’m eager to take on new challenges, learn from every opportunity, and bring valuable insights to the table.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio—I hope you enjoy exploring my work as much as I’ve enjoyed creating it!
